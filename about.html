About Colour Extractor

Welcome to Colour Extractor, a web application designed to help you extract colors from images!

How to Use the App

To extract colors from an image, follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on the "Upload Image" button.
  2. Select an image file from your computer.
  3. Wait for the image to upload and color extraction process to complete.
  4. Tune the max number of colours and threshold values to get desired results
  5. View the extracted colors displayed below the uploaded image.
  6. Copy the colours by clicking on them as RGB or HEX and/or export them as a .txt file.
